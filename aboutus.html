About Us

Established in 1998, Faris Rice Mill has been a cornerstone of quality and reliability in Kurunegala's rice industry for over two decades. Strategically situated in the heart of Kurunegala, our mill has become synonymous with premium quality rice and unparalleled customer service.

Our Legacy Since our inception, we have remained committed to upholding the highest standards of rice production. Our journey began with a vision to deliver the finest rice grains to households across Sri Lanka, ensuring every meal is a testament to purity and taste. Over the years, our dedication to quality has earned us the trust and loyalty of countless customers in Kurunegala and beyond..

Unmatched Quality At Faris Rice Mill, quality is not just a commitment; it's our way of life. We source the finest paddy grains from local farmers, meticulously selecting each batch for its purity and freshness. Our state-of-the-art milling facilities, coupled with stringent quality control measures, ensure that every grain that leaves our mill meets the highest standards of excellence.

Customer-Centric Approach We take pride in our customer-centric approach, striving to exceed expectations at every turn. Whether you're a household consumer or a wholesale partner, our dedicated team is here to serve you with integrity and professionalism. From personalized assistance to timely deliveries, we go above and beyond to ensure your satisfaction.

A Pillar of the Community Faris Rice Mill isn't just a business; it's a part of the fabric of Kurunegala's community. We are deeply invested in the well-being of our employees, farmers, and local residents. Through sustainable practices and community initiatives, we strive to make a positive impact on society and the environment.

Awards and Recognition Our relentless pursuit of excellence has garnered accolades and recognition within the industry. We are proud to have been voted the best rice shop in Kurunegala year after year, a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality and service
